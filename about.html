About Metro Grace International Church

Discover our history, beliefs, and commitment to serving God and our community

Our Story

Metro Grace International Church was founded with a vision to create a welcoming community where people from all walks of life can experience God's transforming love. Since our beginning, we have been committed to sharing the Gospel message and serving our local community with compassion and excellence.

Our church family is diverse, vibrant, and united in our love for Jesus Christ. We believe that everyone has a place at God's table, and we strive to create an atmosphere where authentic relationships can flourish and spiritual growth can happen naturally.

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and our final authority for faith and practice.

Trinity

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Salvation

We believe that salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone, not by works.

Church

We believe the church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, discipleship, and service.

Our Vision & Values

Love

We demonstrate Christ's love through our actions and relationships with one another and our community.

Grace

We extend grace to all people, recognizing that we are all in need of God's forgiveness and mercy.

Truth

We are committed to teaching and living according to God's Word with integrity and authenticity.

Service

We serve others both within our church family and in our broader community as an expression of our faith.
